Creating Connection Through Music

Based in Denver and proud to call it home, DJ Nurture — also known as Jordan White — brings a unique blend of intuition, positive energy, and connection to every performance. With music as the universal language, DJ Nurture turns every event into a shared experience that leaves people smiling, dancing, and connected.

About DJ Nurture

From dancing in the living room to MC Hammer as a kid to creating mixtapes and becoming the go-to Bluetooth hero for friends, Jordan's love for music has always been a defining part of his life. As DJ Nurture, this lifelong passion takes center stage. Professionally trained at the Denver DJ School and mentored by local legends DJ Chonz and DJ Mars, Jordan blends his technical skill with an intuitive ability to connect with a crowd and elevate the energy in any space.

The name DJ Nurture reflects more than just his music; it embodies his approach to life as a credentialed life and leadership coach, bartender, and host of The Practicing Peoples Podcast. Every set he plays is crafted to create a warm and nurturing environment where people feel uplifted, connected to themselves and others, and inspired to dance.
